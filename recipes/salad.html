Salad Recipe

Description

Embark on a journey of freshness and vibrant flavors with this delightful Strawberry Salad featuring a tantalizing Poppy Seed Dressing. Begin by assembling a symphony of crisp mixed greens, including baby spinach and arugula, providing a perfect canvas for the star of the show: succulent, ruby-red strawberries. These sweet jewels, sliced to perfection, infuse the salad with a burst of natural sweetness, complementing the earthy greens. To elevate the ensemble, add a crunch with candied pecans and a creamy note with crumbled feta cheese, creating a harmonious blend of textures that dance on the palate.

The pièce de résistance of this salad is undoubtedly the Poppy Seed Dressing. A luscious concoction of olive oil, balsamic vinegar, honey, and, of course, poppy seeds, this dressing weaves a tapestry of flavors that ties the salad together. Its slightly sweet and tangy profile drapes the ingredients with a light, refreshing embrace. This Strawberry Salad with Poppy Seed Dressing is not merely a dish; it's a celebration of seasonal freshness and culinary artistry, inviting you to relish each bite and savor the essence of summer on your plate.

Ingredients

  • Salad
    • 1/4 cup sugar
    • 1/3 cup slivered almonds
    • 1 bunch romaine, torn (about 8 cups)
    • 1 small onion, halved and thinly sliced
    • 2 cups halved fresh strawberries
  • Dressing
    • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
    • 2 tablespoons sugar
    • 1 tablespoon sour cream
    • 1 tablespoon 2% milk
    • 2-1/4 teaspoons cider vinegar
    • 1-1/2 teaspoons poppy seeds

Steps

  1. Place sugar in a small heavy skillet; cook and stir over medium-low heat until melted and caramel-colored, about 10 minutes. Stir in almonds until coated. Spread on foil to cool.
  2. Place romaine, onion and strawberries in a large bowl. Whisk together dressing ingredients; toss with salad. Break candied almonds into pieces; sprinkle over salad. Serve immediately.
