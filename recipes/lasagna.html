Lasagna Recipe

Description

Savor the essence of Italian comfort cuisine with this delectable lasagna recipe that promises a symphony of flavors and textures. Embark on a culinary journey by first crafting a robust meat sauce, where ground beef and Italian sausage mingle with sautéed onions and garlic, harmonizing in a tangy tomato medley. Meanwhile, prepare a velvety béchamel sauce that will infuse each layer with a luxurious creaminess.

Assembling this culinary masterpiece is a delightful orchestration of tender lasagna noodles, savory meat sauce, and smooth béchamel, each layer contributing to the crescendo of taste. The finale comes with a generous sprinkle of melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses, creating a golden-brown crust that crowns the dish. Baked to perfection, this lasagna invites you to revel in its savory layers, offering a comforting and gratifying experience that captures the heart of traditional Italian cooking.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 lb Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (28 oz each) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    • In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
    • Add chopped onions and minced garlic, sauté until softened.
    • Add ground beef and Italian sausage, breaking them apart with a spoon, and cook until browned.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  3. Cook the Lasagna Noodles:
    • Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  4. Prepare the Béchamel Sauce:
    • In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
    • Stir in flour to create a roux, cooking for 1-2 minutes.
    • Gradually whisk in the milk, ensuring a smooth consistency.
    • Add nutmeg and continue to cook, stirring, until the sauce thickens. Remove from heat.
  5. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • In a large baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce.
    • Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    • Add a portion of the meat sauce, followed by a layer of béchamel sauce. Sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
    • Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a generous layer of cheese on top.
  6. Bake:
    • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  7. Serve:
    • Allow the lasagna to cool for a few minutes before slicing.
    • Garnish with fresh basil if desired and serve hot.
